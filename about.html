About Soteria Network (SOTER)

Your trusted cryptocurrency for empowering holding and investments, safeguarding with cutting-edge technology. Building the future of Web3 infrastructure with GPU & CPU friendly PoW blockchain technology.

Coin Specifications

General Information

  • Network Type: Layer 1. Independent blockchain network
  • Coin Name: Soteria
  • Ticker Symbol: SOTER
  • Algorithms: Dual Algo: SoterG (GPU) & SoterC (CPU)
  • Block Size: 3 MiB max
  • TX Size: 2 MiB max
  • Maximum Supply: Depends on supply & demand
  • Codebase: Completely adapted to handle 10s block time
  • Blockchain: UTXO & ACCOUNT model
  • FORK ID: 100, activated from Genesis
  • TX Fee: 0.01 Soter/kb
  • TX Confirmation: 60
  • Smart Contract: Still in testnet using Lua VM
  • Sync Time: Improved upto 3 times than other traditional blockchains
  • Ports: P2P Port: 8323, RPC Port: 7896
  • Node: Min 4GiB RAM, Recommended 8GiB RAM
  • Dividend: Will be paid quarterly to donators, backers, investors and holders

Block Reward

  • Block Reward: 0.18 Soter/Block
  • Block Time: 15 Seconds average
  • Orphan Rates: Decreased upto 60% than other blockchains that have the same block time
  • Miners Reward: 0.054 Soter/Block
  • Nodes Reward: 0.0036 Soter/Block
  • Miners Emission: ~310 daily, ~9300 monthly, ~113K yearly, this is valid only for 1st year
  • Nodes Emission: ~20 daily, ~600 monthly, ~7300 yearly, this is valid only for 1st year
  • Dev Emission: ~52 daily, ~1555 monthly, ~18920 yearly, this is valid only for 1st year
  • Difficulity: LWMA3-EMA, retarget at each 6 and 180 blocks
  • Dev Fee: 0.009 Soter/Block
  • Coinbase Maturity: 5000 blocks / ~20 hours
  • Merged Mining: Activated at 2M block height
  • Halving: Mining reward will be reduced over time by either going to POS or introducing merged mining to reduce selling pressure and inflation.
  • Total Emission: ~1000 daily, ~30K monthly, ~365K yearly, this is valid only for 1st year
  • Burning Mechanism: Algorithmic burning mechanism will be activated at 2M height with a burning rate 2-3% quarterly
